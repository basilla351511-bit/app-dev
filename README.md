# My Favorite Series: Ginny & Georgia

## Why I Love It
- Complex mother-daughter dynamics  
- Smart blend of drama and humor  
- Tackles real-life issues like identity, trauma, and privilege  

## Favorite Characters
1. Georgia Miller – fierce, flawed, and unforgettable  
2. Ginny Miller – navigating adolescence with raw honesty  
3. Maxine – vibrant and unapologetically herself
